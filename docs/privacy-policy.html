Privacy Policy

AUN - ReqStudio · Last updated: April 23, 2026

In short: AUN - ReqStudio stores your API workspace data locally on your device. If you sign in, Google, Apple, and Firebase process limited account data to authenticate you. The app uses Google AdMob to display ads, which may process device and ad-related data to serve and measure advertising.

About AUN - ReqStudio

AUN - ReqStudio is an API testing and HTTP client application for iOS and Android, developed by Maulik Raja. It allows you to build and send HTTP requests, test WebSocket connections, manage request collections, and inspect API responses from your phone or tablet.

Data We Collect

AUN - ReqStudio stores the data you create in the app locally on your device — including API requests, collections, headers, authentication credentials, request history, and responses. We do not operate our own analytics backend for this app. When you use optional sign-in, account-related data is processed by Google, Apple, and Google Firebase as described below. The app uses Google AdMob for advertising; Google may process device, advertising, diagnostic, and interaction data as described in Google's own policies.

Sign-in and account data

Optional sign-in is provided through Firebase Authentication. Depending on the method you choose:

  • Sign in with Google: Google's sign-in flow may share an ID token and basic profile information (such as your email address and name, as permitted by your Google account) with Firebase to create or access your app account.
  • Sign in with Apple (iOS): Apple's authentication service may share tokens and, where you allow it, your name or email with Firebase to create or access your app account.

Firebase issues a user identifier and session tokens used by the app. A session identifier may be stored in secure storage on your device for app features (for example, preferences tied to your session). We do not use sign-in to read the contents of your collections or requests on our servers; that content remains on your device unless you use optional export or backup features you control.

These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies:

  • Firebase Privacy Information
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

Data Storage

Your data is stored in the following ways:

  • Local device storage: All app data (collections, history, settings) is stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage.
  • iOS Keychain: Sensitive information such as authentication tokens and credentials are stored securely in the iOS Keychain, which is protected by your device's hardware encryption.
  • iCloud Documents (optional): If you choose to use the iCloud backup feature, a copy of your collections and settings is saved to your personal iCloud Documents container. This data is encrypted by Apple and synced only to your own iCloud account. We have no access to this data.

Device Permissions

AUN - ReqStudio may request the following permissions:

  • Photo Library (read): Used only when you choose to attach a file or image to an API request body. We never access your photos without your explicit action.
  • Photo Library (write): Used only when you choose to save an exported file or response to your photo library.
  • Local Notifications: Used to alert you when long-running API requests complete. These are local notifications only — no data is sent to any server.

Network Activity

AUN - ReqStudio sends HTTP/HTTPS requests and establishes WebSocket connections only to the URLs that you explicitly configure. The app also connects to Google AdMob services to request and display ads. The app requires unrestricted network access (NSAllowsArbitraryLoads) because, as an API testing tool, it must be able to reach any server you specify, including non-HTTPS endpoints.

Third-Party Services

AUN - ReqStudio integrates Google AdMob to display advertising (including banner, native, interstitial, and rewarded formats where enabled). Google may collect and process data in accordance with its own privacy policies, including information used for ad delivery, fraud prevention, reporting, and measurement. Learn more here:

  • How Google uses data from apps that use its services

Data Sharing

We do not sell your in-app content such as collections, requests, environments, or history entries. Ad-related data may be processed by Google through AdMob to provide advertising services.

Children's Privacy

AUN - ReqStudio is a developer tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
