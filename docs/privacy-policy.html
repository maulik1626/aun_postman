Privacy Policy

AUN - ReqStudio · Last updated: April 22, 2026

In short: AUN - ReqStudio stores your request data locally on your device. The app also uses Google AdMob to display ads, which may process limited device and ad-related data to serve and measure advertising.

About AUN - ReqStudio

AUN - ReqStudio is an API testing and HTTP client application for iOS, developed by Maulik Raja. It allows you to build and send HTTP requests, test WebSocket connections, manage request collections, and inspect API responses — all from your iPhone or iPad.

Data We Collect

AUN - ReqStudio stores the data you create in the app locally on your device — including API requests, collections, headers, authentication credentials, request history, and responses. We do not operate our own analytics backend for this app. However, the app uses Google AdMob, and Google may process device, advertising, diagnostic, and interaction data as described in Google's own policies.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in the following ways:

  • Local device storage: All app data (collections, history, settings) is stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage.
  • iOS Keychain: Sensitive information such as authentication tokens and credentials are stored securely in the iOS Keychain, which is protected by your device's hardware encryption.
  • iCloud Documents (optional): If you choose to use the iCloud backup feature, a copy of your collections and settings is saved to your personal iCloud Documents container. This data is encrypted by Apple and synced only to your own iCloud account. We have no access to this data.

Device Permissions

AUN - ReqStudio may request the following permissions:

  • Photo Library (read): Used only when you choose to attach a file or image to an API request body. We never access your photos without your explicit action.
  • Photo Library (write): Used only when you choose to save an exported file or response to your photo library.
  • Local Notifications: Used to alert you when long-running API requests complete. These are local notifications only — no data is sent to any server.

Network Activity

AUN - ReqStudio sends HTTP/HTTPS requests and establishes WebSocket connections only to the URLs that you explicitly configure. The app also connects to Google AdMob services to request and display ads. The app requires unrestricted network access (NSAllowsArbitraryLoads) because, as an API testing tool, it must be able to reach any server you specify, including non-HTTPS endpoints.

Third-Party Services

AUN - ReqStudio integrates Google AdMob to display advertising. Google may collect and process data in accordance with its own privacy policies, including information used for ad delivery, fraud prevention, reporting, and measurement. Learn more here:

  • How Google uses data from apps that use its services

Data Sharing

We do not sell your in-app content such as collections, requests, environments, or history entries. Ad-related data may be processed by Google through AdMob to provide advertising services.

Children's Privacy

AUN - ReqStudio is a developer tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
